Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is EXO having a comeback 2022?

    According to an unofficial update, EXO is going to make a full group comeback in 2023, in January or February.

  • What made EXO famous?

    The groups came together in 2013 to release their debut album, XOXO, and the album's repackage released later in the year saw EXO's popularity hit a fever pitch with the funky, dance-pop single “Growl.”

  • When did EXO get popular?

    EXO debuted in 2011 under SM entertainment and while they didn't receive popularity immediately, they hit it BIG with their song Growl, released in 2013. Since then they just catapulted to success and became the nation's boy group, won multiple awards and daesangs, and became the most popular boy group.

  • Who designed all EXO logo?

    Chanyeol invented EXO's hexagon logo!

  • When did EXO win their first Daesang award?

    Exo's first studio album XOXO was released in June 2013 and received various awards, including their first Daesang award.
