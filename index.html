XIUMIN The 1st Mini Album 'Brand New'

EXO member Xiumin has released his long-awaited debut mini-album ‘Brand New’, along with a music video for its lead single of the same name.

On September 26 at 6pm KST, the EXO vocalist dropped the music video for ‘Brand New’, the title track for his debut mini-album which arrived at the same time.

In the new music video, Xiumin time travels through the different seasons throughout the year, from a snowy winter night to an outdoor picnic on a bright summer day. “Wake up the new me / Call you, oh, you / It’s too early to be surprised / Baby, let it loose, let it loose,” he sings on the groovy chorus.

With the arrival of ‘Brand New’, Xiumin is now the seventh member of nine-piece K-pop boyband EXO to go solo. He follows bandmates Baekhyun, Chen, Suho, Lay, Kai and D.O in doing so.

Prior to this release, Xiumin has participated in a number of unofficial solo projects in the years since. These include his SM Station project single ‘You’, as well as numerous original soundtracks to K-dramas throughout the years, including 2020’s Mr. Queen and Falling For Challenge in 2015.

Chanyeol returns from military service

  • Exo member Chanyeol has been discharged from his mandatory military service.
  • The singer enlisted in March 2021.
  • He is yet to make any post on social media.

EXO's Chanyeol has been discharged from his mandatory military service as of September 28, 2022.

The idol, who enlisted for his mandatory duties as an active duty soldier in March of 2021, carried out a large portion of his mandatory duties as a lead actor for the army musical 'Blue Helmet: A Song of Meissa' alongside stars like Jang Ki Yong, ONF's Hyojin, and more.

Chanyeol is now the 5th member of EXO to successfully complete his mandatory military service. Fellow member Baekhyun is scheduled to be discharged from his duties as a public service worker in February of 2023.
